Our History

"The battle of building a business is only known to those who have tried to build one"
2006

The Entrepreneurial Battle

In 2006, a coffee roasting business launched in Massachusetts. Despite an MBA and 12 years of finance experience, the founder was drowning. Three years in, the "dream" had become a struggle of spreadsheets and backlogged books.

The Turning Point

The Alice Effect

Alice stepped in and transformed chaos into clarity. She didn't just do bookkeeping; she built an ecosystem by integrating QuickBooks, merchant services, and monthly closings that allowed the founder to finally lead with vision instead of fear.

Present

The Birth of IKAN

After years of building her business independently, Alice partnered with Peter to establish IKAN Business Partners — combining their expertise to deliver broader services while creating opportunities for talented individuals dedicated to exceptional financial stewardship
