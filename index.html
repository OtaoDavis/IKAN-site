The IKAN Origin

From regular staff accountant to
a small-medium business financial rescuer

Alice didn’t set out to build a systems focused company — it all began in 2008 inside a small coffee roasting shop in Massachusetts.

The owner, Peter, was managing everything through spreadsheets: inventory, customer and vendor list, sales, and financials. It was time consuming, error prone, and difficult to scale. Alice suggested implementing an accounting system.

She installed QuickBooks Desktop and integrated it seamlessly with the store’s website and its merchant account.

That changed everything. It became easier to track inventory, manage orders and generate sales, and other financial reports. Synchronizing the sales from the merchant account to the bank and accounting system, eliminated a lot of manual work, enabling Peter to focus on growing his business.

It’s not just about software. It’s about empowering business owners to reclaim their time, strengthen their operations, and finally run their businesses.
